Investment Criteria

What Defines the Right Opportunity

Our most successful partnerships tend to align with these principles.

Investment Size

We typically invest between $500K-$5M of minority equity, with a preferred range of $1-3M per deal.

Geography

Our search is hyper-local - Denver metro, Boulder, and within a 50-mile radius. We want to be close to what we build.

ETA Model

We are open to all ETA models, including traditional search funds, self-funded searchers, committed capital vehicles, long-term holds, and independent sponsors.

Industry Focus

We focus on recession-resilient, essential service businesses - home services, commercial cleaning, pest control, fire safety, landscaping, traffic control, home healthcare, and skilled trades. Businesses that communities depend on regardless of the economy.

Business Profile

We look for bootstrapped, owner-operated businesses with 1-100 employees, $750K-$10M EBITDA, and $1M-$50M in revenue. No prior PE or VC - we prefer businesses that have grown organically under strong ownership.

Deal Structure

We look for an attractive deal price and structure with a clear alignment of incentives.

Buyer Edge

Seeking a credible, compelling right to win.

Cap Table

For traditional search funds, we target a cap table allocation of 5-15%.
