OUR STORY

We Grew Up Around Family Businesses, Too.

Route 62 Holdings was founded by Max and Nicole - two partners who grew up close to family-owned businesses and spent years working alongside the owners who built them.

The more we sat across from those owners, the more we realized the hardest question wasn’t price or terms - it was who would look after what they’d spent a lifetime creating.

That’s what we’re here for. We step in as hands-on operators, keep the team and culture the owner built, and carry the business forward - the way family would.
